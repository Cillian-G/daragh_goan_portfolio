About Daragh

Daragh Goan is a Dublin based writer and director. He obtained a first class honors in Film & Television Production from the National Film School in IADT in 2020, majoring in directing and minoring in producing. He won Best Screenplay at the Royal Television Society Republic of Ireland Student Awards in 2020.
